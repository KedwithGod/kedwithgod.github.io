Who we are

Croon helps you connect with your favorite stars and give back to charity. It’s a double whammy.

Get access to thousands of superstars and recieve a customized video according to your guidelines. From there they’ll donante some or all of the moneies received to a registered charity of their choice.

It’s a great way to keep the money going back to good causes and to help you get videos you need to feel awesome. It’s a win-win. We’re putting good back into the rotation, and you’re at the helm.

How We work

Our Giving Back To Humanity Mechanism

Find the Perfect Star

Whether it’s a birthday, anniversary, or a roast for your friend, the superstar will deliver, They’ll be ready to record a personalized video on request.

Book your Superstar

With the app, request the star of your choice and tell them what you’d like to include in video. They’ll get back to you within a week unless you’ve booked the, for a 24 hour turnaround.

Receive your Personalized
Message

Sit back and enjoy as a star records a customized message meant for you.

Gift it Back

The Star donates some or all of the earnings to a registered charity around the world. Take a video of you or of your friend receiving the superstar video and tag us to get extra points redeemable as future discounts.
